ABOUT OUR CLINIC

Dedicated to Your Well-Being

Founded in 2009, MediCare Clinic has been a cornerstone of community healthcare for over 15 years. Our state-of-the-art facility combines modern technology with a warm, welcoming environment where patients feel truly cared for.

We believe healthcare should be accessible, transparent, and centered around you. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, our multidisciplinary team works collaboratively to deliver the best outcomes.

WHAT WE OFFER

Our Services

From routine wellness visits to specialized treatments, we provide a full spectrum of medical services under one roof.

General Medicine

Comprehensive health assessments, preventive care, and management of chronic conditions.

  • Annual check-ups
  • Chronic disease management
  • Preventive screenings

Pediatrics

Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents in a child-friendly environment.

  • Well-child visits
  • Vaccinations
  • Growth monitoring

Orthopedics

Expert diagnosis and treatment for musculoskeletal injuries and conditions.

  • Fracture care
  • Joint replacement
  • Sports medicine

Neurology

Advanced neurological evaluations and personalized treatment plans.

  • Headache management
  • Epilepsy care
  • Nerve conduction studies

Ophthalmology

Complete eye care from routine exams to advanced surgical procedures.

  • Vision testing
  • Cataract surgery
  • Glaucoma treatment

Cardiology

State-of-the-art cardiac diagnostics and cardiovascular health management.

  • ECG & Echocardiography
  • Heart failure care
  • Risk assessment
